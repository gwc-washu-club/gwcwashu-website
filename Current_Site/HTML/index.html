Our goal is to empower young women to explore computer science and to help reduce the gender gap in the growing tech industry.

The problem

The tech industry is growing rapidly, but girls are being left behind. Even though interest in computer science is high in younger girls, that interest ebbs as girls grow older, with the biggest drop off being between the ages of 12 and 17.

Information from girlswhocode.com
