Join as a Participant

We're so excited for your interest in Girls Who Code WashU!

Girls Who Code WashU is completely free for all students, and a laptop is appreciated, but not required. We encourage anyone who is concerned about transportation to reach out to us, as we have a small amount of money available for transportation subsidies. If you filled out the intersted form over the summer, you will be receiving an email with the sign up form soon.

Sadly, we are not hosting our WashU clubs this semester. However, if you attend STEAM Academy, KIPP Triumph, or Brittany Woods and want to know how to get involved, or if you are interested in starting a club at your school, please email us at communications.gwcwashu@gmail.com.
